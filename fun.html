Home About Reviews Gallery Fun Facts

1. Capybaras can remain under water for up to 5 minutes

2. Capybaras can reach running speeds of 22mph!

3. An average group of capybaras is between 5-15 adults big. However, groups as large as 60 adults have been spotted!

4. Capybaras can communicate in infrasonic and ultrasonic sound frequencies, meaning they can hear sounds both above and below what the human ear can hear.

5. Capybaras can sing!
