About the Capybara

Home About Reviews Gallery Fun Facts

Capybaras are herbivores, meaning that they will only eat plant material. Their diet is made mainly up of grasses and aquatic plants, as well as fruits, berries, and tree bark.

They are highly sociable animals. They do the best in large groups, meaning that you should never own just one capybara.

Capybara's have a life span of 8-10 years. However, in the while, they usually only live under four years because they are the favorite prey of the jaguar, anaconda, puma, ocelot, and eagle.
